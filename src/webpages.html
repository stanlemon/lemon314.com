In 1995 Microsoft released FrontPage, a WYSIWYG web design tool that made it possible for non-technical users to create and maintain websites. While Dad was technical, he still loved the tool and began building websites for himself and the community he loved living in. He became prolific in retirement building sites, WegoWeb.net being the most popular, and then WegoNews.com.
